## 📋 Frontmatter Field Guide

- **title**: Clear, compelling title (50-60 characters ideal)
- **slug**: URL-friendly version (lowercase, hyphens, no spaces)
- **description**: Meta description for SEO (150-160 characters)
- **category**: Choose from: Pet Care, Health, Nutrition, Grooming, Training, Adoption Stories
- **featuredImage**: High-quality image URL (1200x630px recommended)
- **author**: Your name or "Paltuu Team"
- **date**: Publication date in YYYY-MM-DD format
- **tags**: 5-10 specific tags (lowercase, hyphen-separated)

## 🏷️ Tag Examples

Good tags:
- "puppy-training-tips"
- "cat-nutrition-guide"
- "karachi-pet-adoption"
- "summer-pet-safety"
- "vet-checkup-pakistan"

Avoid:
- "pets" (too generic)
- "animals" (too broad)
- "tips" (not specific enough)
